The Bon Appetit Problem Day-3

Problem

HackerRank Problem

Two friends Anna and Brian, are deciding how to split the bill at a dinner. Each will only pay for the items they consume. Brian gets the check and calculates Anna's portion. You must determine if his calculation is correct.

We are given the amount of money that Brian charged Anna And the bill which is an array of integers(prices) Also we are given the item that Anna doesn't eat and needs to be subtracted.

Solution

Picture/Diagram

Pseudocode

        correctSplit = sum of array - the item Anna did not eat

        IF the money Anna was charged - correctSplit === 0;
            THEN print  “Bon Appetit!!”
        ELSE  
            return Money owed to Anna
